1. Library A library is an organized collection of information resources—including books, films, and digital materials—that provides access to knowledge and services for its users. Derived from the Latin word "liber" meaning "book," libraries serve as institutions that house, organize, and preserve these materials to support study, research, and lifelong learning for individuals and communities. Library
2. PlayGround A playground is an important facility that allows children to engage in sports and physical activities. It should provide enough open space for games like football, basketball, or cricket, along with safe play equipment such as swings and slides for younger kids. Playgrounds not only improve physical health but also promote teamwork and stress relief. PlayGround
3. ClassRooms Classrooms are the primary learning spaces where children spend most of their time in school. They should be spacious, well-ventilated, and equipped with proper seating arrangements to ensure comfort and focus. Good lighting, colorful charts, and boards create an engaging environment that supports effective learning. ClassRooms
4. Cafeteria Cafeteria or canteen in schools provides students with nutritious and hygienic meals. The food served should be balanced, fresh, and healthy to support children’s growth, while avoiding junk food. Proper seating and clean surroundings make the cafeteria a pleasant space for students to enjoy their meals and refresh themselves. Cafeteria
5. Science Laboratories Well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to provide hands-on learning experiences. Science Laboratories

Safety

Sl No Items Description Image Link
1. CCTV Safety in school is one of the most important aspects of a child’s education, as children can only learn and grow in a secure environment. Schools must ensure the safety of kids through proper infrastructure and strict measures. Security systems like CCTV cameras, security guards, and safe entry and exit points help prevent any unauthorized access. Classrooms and corridors should be well-maintained to avoid accidents, with fire extinguishers and alarms installed in case of emergencies. Safe and hygienic transportation, such as buses with attendants and seat belts, ensures children travel securely to and from school. CCTV
2. Medical and First Aid Facilities A medical room or first aid center is essential to handle health issues and minor injuries immediately. It should have basic medicines, a first aid kit, and a trained nurse or medical staff. Quick medical attention builds trust among parents and ensures that students are safe and cared for inside the school premises. Medical and First Aid Facilities
3. Hygiene and Health Safety Clean drinking water, hygienic restrooms, and handwashing facilities are part of school safety. Proper sanitation prevents the spread of diseases and keeps children healthy. Regular cleaning, sanitization, and health awareness programs teach students the importance of personal hygiene and healthy practices. Hygiene and Health Safety
